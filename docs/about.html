About Our Restaurant

Patty's Burger Restaurant started as a small family-owned business focused on serving fresh, affordable food to our community.

Our specialties include burgers, pizza, fries, and homemade meals prepared with quality ingredients.

Our mission is to provide good food and a welcoming environment for everyone.
